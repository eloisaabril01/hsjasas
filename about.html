About Cargo Express

Two decades of excellence in global logistics, connecting businesses worldwide with innovative transportation solutions

Our Story

Established in 2014, Cargo Express Logistics (CEL) began as a vision to revolutionize global trade through innovative logistics solutions. We are a leading Freight Forwarder, NVOCC, and Multimodal Transport Operator, recognized globally for our excellence in comprehensive logistics services.

We are globally recognized for our excellence in Container Freight Forwarding, Customs Clearance, DDP/DAP shipment handling, Air Freight, Flat Rack & ODC Cargo handling, and comprehensive port logistics. CEL operates across all major global shipping routes and ports, offering end-to-end logistics and transportation services.

We cater to all key Indian ports including Nhava Sheva (Mumbai), Mundra, Hazira, Pipavav, Chennai, Tuticorin, Kolkata, Kochi, and all major ICDs across India, ensuring seamless connectivity for businesses across the subcontinent and beyond.
